Diagnosis and Monitoring of MGUS

MGUS Management

  • M protein anything other than IgG
  • FLC ratio outside of 0.25 - 1.65
  • M protein > 15  (or > 3 for CKD)

If Any of these risk factors present, need further testing...

  • IgM -> look for plasmacytoma/lymphadenopathy
  • Light chains -> look for amyloidosis in kidney and heart
